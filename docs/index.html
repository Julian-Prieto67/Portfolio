ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Julian Prieto, and I'm a recent graduate from the Master of Engineering Mechanical Engineering program at Cornell University. To me, robotics embodies the intersection of creativity, problem-solving, and engineering. It requires people to identify challenges in the world, systematically develop solutions, and then iterate until the design is both elegant and effective. I love that blend of theory and hands-on work and nothing makes me happier than creating a well designed system.
